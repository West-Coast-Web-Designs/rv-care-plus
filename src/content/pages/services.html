Our Services

At RV Care Plus, we provide a comprehensive range of mobile RV repair and maintenance services, tailored to meet the unique needs of RV owners in Colorado Springs. Whether your RV is stationary or on the road, our skilled technicians are here to keep your home and investment in top condition.

Preventive Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to extending the life of your RV and preventing costly repairs. Our preventive services include:

  • Roof inspections and sealing
  • Appliance maintenance (water heaters, refrigerators, air conditioners)
  • Plumbing system checks and repairs
  • Electrical system diagnostics

On-Site Repairs

We bring our expertise directly to you, offering fast and reliable repairs for:

  • Heating and cooling systems
  • Slide-outs and leveling systems
  • Water leaks and plumbing issues
  • Appliances and electrical systems

General Handyman Services

Sometimes, your RV just needs small fixes to keep things running smoothly. We handle general repairs like:

  • Fixing cabinet hinges or drawers
  • Replacing light fixtures or bulbs
  • Adjusting doors and windows
  • Minor carpentry and hardware fixes

Tank Cleaning and Sanitization

Maintain a clean and healthy RV with professional black and grey water tank cleaning and sanitization services.

Specialized Exterior Services

Protect your RV from the elements with our exterior services, including:

  • Roof sealing and repairs
  • Slide-out maintenance
  • Awning repairs and replacements

Full Inspections

Whether you’re preparing for a trip or ensuring your stationary RV stays in top shape, we provide thorough inspections to identify and resolve potential issues.
